# Mobile-Sales-Dashboard

The Mobile Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze mobile phone sales performance across multiple dimensions such as time, brand, mobile model, payment method, and customer behavior.
This dashboard helps stakeholders quickly understand sales trends, revenue performance, customer preferences, and comparisons with the previous year to support data-driven decision-making.
The project uses structured sales data CSV file and converts raw transactional records into meaningful business insights using Power BI visualizations.

1. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the most important business metrics at the top:
Total Sales – Overall revenue generated from mobile sales
Total Quantity – Total number of units sold
Transactions – Number of completed sales transactions
Average Price – Average selling price per mobile device 

2. Filters & Slicers

The dashboard provides multiple interactive filters:
Mobile Model
Brand
Payment Method
Year / Quarter / Month / Day
Month Selection Panel

3. Visualizations & Insights
   3.1 Total Quantity by Month (Line Chart) :-
   Displays monthly sales quantity trends.
   Helps identify seasonality and peak demand periods.
   Useful for inventory and supply chain planning.

   3.2 Customer Ratings by Rating Status (Bar Chart) :-
   Categorizes customer feedback into Good, Average, and Poor.
   Provides insight into customer satisfaction and product quality.
   Helps businesses identify areas for service or product improvement.

   3.3 Transactions by Payment Method (Donut Chart) :-
   UPI
   Debit Card
   Credit Card
   Cash

   3.4 Brand Performance Table :-
   Displays Total Sales and Transactions for each brand.
   Enables quick comparison between major mobile brands.
   Helps identify top-performing and underperforming brands.

   3.5 Total Sales by Mobile Model (Horizontal Bar Chart) :-
   Highlights top revenue-generating mobile models.
   Assists in product demand analysis and marketing strategy.

   3.6 Total Sales by Day Name (Area Chart) :-
   Analyzes sales distribution across weekdays.
   Useful for identifying high-sales days and planning promotions.

4. MTD (Month-to-Date) Analysis :-
Shows cumulative sales growth for the selected month.
Helps track daily progress against monthly targets.
Useful for real-time performance monitoring.   

5. Same Period Last Year Comparison
Comparison by Year, Quarter, and Month.
Identifies growth trends or declines.
Helps evaluate business performance consistency over time.
